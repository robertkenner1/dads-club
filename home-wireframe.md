## Section-by-section notes

### Nav
- Wordmark left in the display serif (small, ~20px height).
- Three text links (About, Events, Contact) + the **Join** CTA on the right.
- Join is the only nav item with a hover-underline in the terracotta accent + caret.
- At S the three links collapse to `☰`; **Join stays visible outside the menu** — it's the primary conversion.
- Sticky on scroll? Recommend **no** for this direction — it adds visual noise. Reconsider if user-testing shows people lose the CTA mid-scroll.

### Hero
- Asymmetric: headline left (≈4 cols), photo right (≈7 cols), 1 col gutter.
- Headline is the mission line set as Display serif; not centered, ranged left, narrow measure.
- Eyebrow "EST. 2025" in small-caps sans above the headline.
- CTA below the headline as a text link with caret. No button fill.
- Caption sits under the photo in muted sans — names the location and season.
- Hero photo: `sunset-dock-gathering.jpeg` is the recommended choice; alternates: `pickleball-team-group.jpeg`.
- Mobile: photo stacks above headline (visual hook first), caption between, body + CTA below.

### The Why
- Indented from the left edge — sits roughly in cols 2–7. Right side breathes.
- Eyebrow "THE WHY" in small-caps sans.
- Tagline ("Built by Dads, for Dads.") in display serif, smaller than hero.
- Body sets the first paragraph of the About long-form copy (from `content.md` §3).
- "Read the full story →" links to `/about`. No image in this section — it's a typographic moment.

### Next Gathering
- Single event, pulled from Wix Events as the next upcoming instance.
- Eyebrow "NEXT GATHERING" left-aligned.
- Event name = serif H2 with a hairline rule under it.
- Date · time · location on one line in muted sans, separated by middle dots.
- Two text-link CTAs: RSVP (→ Wix Events URL for that event) and See all events (→ `/events`).
- If no upcoming event exists (off-season), the section swaps to: "Next gathering is being planned. **Join the list →**" linking to the Google Form.

### In Practice (photo grid)
- Eyebrow "IN PRACTICE" left-aligned.
- Canonical event-description paragraph (from `content.md` §4), indented to match The Why.
- Photo grid: **asymmetric** — one large (≈7 cols) + two smaller stacked (≈4 cols total). Resist the urge to do a tidy 3-up. Editorial > tidy.
- Each photo has a caption below in muted sans.
- Recommended photos: `pickleball-team-group.jpeg` (large), `dad-daughter-pumpkin-festival.jpeg`, `guy-laughing-string-lights.jpeg`.
- Mobile: collapses to single column, full-width images with captions underneath.

### On Instagram
- Eyebrow "ON INSTAGRAM" left, handle "@officialdadsclub →" right (links out).
- Live embed of 6 most recent IG posts in a row at L, 3×2 grid at M, 2×2 grid at S.
- Don't restyle — let IG's thumbnails sit as-is. Hairline border around each at 1px.
- Loads lazily; if the embed fails, section hides itself (don't show broken state).

### Join Our Movement (newsletter)
- Eyebrow + serif sentence + inline field + Sign Up text-link.
- Single-line layout at M/L; stacks at S.
- Success message replaces the form: *"Thanks for submitting!"* in serif italic.
- This is the **on-domain** newsletter — distinct from the Google Form join. Two different lists, two different intents (newsletter = passive interest, Join Dad's Club = active membership).

### Footer
- Wordmark top-left in display serif.
- Three columns at L (Contact / Connect / Site); 2-up at M; stacked at S.
- Hairline rule above the bottom row.
- Bottom row: copyright left, **mascot mark** small in the right corner — this is the one place the beaver lives on Home. Keep it ≤24px equivalent.
- IG glyph appears next to the Instagram link, not as the section icon.

## CTA hierarchy on Home

| Tier | CTA | Appears |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | **Join Dad's Club** (Google Form) | Nav, Hero, Footer (Site col) |
| Secondary | RSVP next gathering | Next Gathering section |
| Secondary | See all events | Next Gathering section |
| Tertiary | Read the full story | The Why → /about |
| Tertiary | @officialdadsclub | Instagram section header |
| Tertiary | Sign Up (newsletter) | Newsletter section |

## Vertical rhythm

Section spacing (between hairline dividers):
- L: 120px top + 120px bottom inside each section
- M: 88px
- S: 64px

Hairline divider color is Rule (`#D9CFBE`), 1px, full-width.

## Things I'd flag before moving to high-fidelity

- **Photo captions need real copy.** Right now they're placeholders. Once you have actual event names/dates per photo, the section will land harder.
- **The "off-season fallback" for Next Gathering** is worth building from day one — events are seasonal and the section shouldn't sit empty.
- **Mascot in footer:** is the existing illustration small enough to read at 24px, or does it need a single-color simplification? Probably the latter.
- **IG embed:** confirm we can style the 1px hairline border around tiles, or accept the default IG embed style.
- **No hero CTA fallback for mobile:** the Join CTA in the nav stays visible at S, so the hero's "Join Dad's Club →" link is the second touch. That's intentional — don't add a third Join button mid-page.
