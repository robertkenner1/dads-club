Dad's Club exists to build belonging, community, and brotherhood among Dads.

Dad's Club was founded by a husband and wife duo who saw a gap—and decided to do something about it. They believed that Dads deserved a place where they could gather, connect, and belong.

We realized that many dads are carrying a lot, often quietly, and not always with the community they deserve. We wanted to change that.

Whether you're a new dad or have been doing this for years, there's a seat at the table for you.

Come as you are.
Leave with connection.
All Dads are welcome.
